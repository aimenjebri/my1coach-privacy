# Privacy Policy for My1Coach

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

## Overview

My1Coach respects your privacy and is designed with a privacy-first approach.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the My1Coach mobile application ("App").

My1Coach operates entirely on your device. The app does not collect personal data, does not operate external servers, and does not transmit user information outside of your device.

Your data remains under your control.

---

# Information We Collect

My1Coach does not collect personal information.

All information you create inside the app — including habits, financial entries, reflections, and mood logs — is stored locally on your device.

The app does not send this information to the developer or to external systems.

Examples of data that remain on your device include:

• Habit tracking data
• Financial entries and spending logs
• Reflections or journal entries
• Mood tracking information
• App preferences

This data is never transmitted to us.

---

# How Your Data Is Stored

My1Coach uses local storage on your device.

The app:

• Stores information locally on your device
• Does not create user accounts
• Does not require sign-in
• Does not upload information to developer-controlled servers
• Does not sync your data to external databases

Your device’s security protections (such as passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID) help safeguard this information.

---

# Analytics and Tracking

My1Coach does not include analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

We do not collect:

• Usage analytics
• Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
• Device identifiers used for tracking
• Location data
• Behavioral tracking data

Your activity within the app is not monitored or analyzed by us.

---

# Apple Diagnostics

If you enable Apple’s device analytics settings (such as “Share iPhone Analytics”), Apple may collect anonymous diagnostic information related to app performance.

This information is collected by Apple and governed by Apple’s privacy policies.

My1Coach does not receive this information.

---

# Notifications

My1Coach may send local notifications to remind you about habits or goals.

These notifications:

• Are generated locally on your device
• Are not sent through external servers
• Are not tracked or monitored by us

You can manage notifications through your device’s settings.

---

# Third-Party Services

My1Coach does not integrate with:

• Third-party analytics platforms
• Advertising networks
• Marketing trackers
• External data collection services

The app uses only standard system frameworks provided by Apple.

---

# Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose personal data.

Because My1Coach does not collect your information, there is no user data available for sharing with third parties.

All information remains on your device.

---

# Your Control Over Your Data

You retain full control over your information.

You may:

• Delete all app data by removing the app from your device
• Include the data in your personal device backups
• Export information using iOS sharing features if available

The developer cannot access or retrieve your data.

---

# Children’s Privacy

My1Coach does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

The app functions entirely on-device without collecting user data.

---

# Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes to the app or applicable requirements.

When updates occur, the "Last Updated" date will be revised.

Continued use of the app after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
